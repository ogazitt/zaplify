zaplify

Welcome to Zaplify!

Zaplify is a new kind of item organizer. While other folder applications merely help you track your to-do list, Zaplify helps you complete your items by making valuable suggestions.

Zaplify is a fully-functional "offline" application - meaning that it works great whether or not you have created an account, and whether or not your phone happens to be connected to the Web.

However, to get the most out of Zaplify, you will want to create a free account. Here are some of the benefits:

  • All of your data will automatically be backed up in the Cloud
  • You can access your data from any device - your computer, a web browser, or your mobile device
  • You will enjoy Zaplify's unique capability of suggesting ways to complete your item

To create your free account, go to the Settings page at any time by tapping the settings button. Don't worry, unless you tell us otherwise, any data you've already created will automatically be sent to the Cloud for safekeeping.

Items

The main tab in the application is the Items tab. This tab provides you a sorted list of all the items in all of you folders. You can filter this page by tapping the filter button. You can also create new items in this tab by tapping the add button. Notice that the first thing you need to tell us is what list you want to add the item to. You can control which list this defaults in the Settings page.

Folders

Folders are a convenient way of organizing your items. To view your folders, swipe to the folders tab. You may have noticed that we've already created two folders for you - To Do and Shopping. You can add a new list by tapping the add button.

If you want to change or delete any of the folders (the ones added automatically or your own folders), you can tap the list name and then tap the edit button. Once you've tapped a list name, you can see the items within that list. Each of the tabs will sort by a different field - by name, by due date, or by priority.

You can add new items from this page, either by tapping the add button, which will let you enter all of the fields of the item, or by tapping the quick add button, which will allow you to rapidly create new items by entering just their name in the dialog and then tapping add. Once you're done, tap done to dismiss the dialog.

List Types

The two lists we've added each have a different list type - To Do List and Shopping List, respectively. These two list types have different styles - the To Do List has more fields that are displayed when you add or edit a item that belongs to a list of this type. The Shopping List has only a Name field and is more appropriate for simple items. Finally, there is another built-in list type called Freeform List - this list type has a big Details field that allows entering a free-form description, which can contain phone numbers, email addresses, websites, and so on.

Items and Actions

When you tap a item, you are placed on the Actions tab, which displays any quick actions associated with that item. For example, if your item has a due date, the available actions are Add to calendar and postpone to tomorrow. If your item has a phone number, a website, or an email address, you will have the appropriate actions to phone, text, navigate, or email.

Tags

Tags are a convenient way to associate a set of related items across lists. You can have zero, one, or as many tags as you want on a Item. Just enter them in, separated by commas. The Tags tab allows you to look at all your tags and bring up all the items that have a particular tag. Tags can also be color-coded to provide a better visual cue. Just tap the tag name in the Tags tab, and then tap the edit button. You can also delete tags from this screen.

Advanced features

  • Templates: You can designate a list as a template by tapping the list, tapping the edit button, and tapping the template checkbox. This list will now be available to import items from. To import a template into a list, just tap the import from template menu item in the list page.
  • Deleting all completed items: You can delete all of the completed items in a list by tapping the delete all completed items menu item in the list page.
  • Email data: You can send a formatted copy of your data to any email address, so you can get your data out of the application at any time.
  • Sync now: Zaplify will automatically sync your items, but you can trigger an immediate sync anytime by tapping the sync now button in the main page.
